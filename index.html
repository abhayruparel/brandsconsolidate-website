Product Testing

At request we can get products tested at NABL certified laboratories

Online Production Inspection

This production inspection ensures continuous inspection of a select sample quantity at predetermined intervals at various stages of production to identify and ensure corrections where required to have a flawless production for all production parameters.
(Our team is present during the production run at the factory 24/7).

Random Production Inspection

This inspection is done after 34 - 36 hours of production – samples are drawn as per AQL Standards and inspected for any delayed movement post production and packing.
This helps to ensure any post production irregularities and capture a random status of materials after production.

Pallet and Label Inspection

This stage is to ensure compliance on specifications of Pallet and Labelling/Barcode/EAN requirements for different countries.

Container Loading Supervision

To ascertain and supervise that all required specifications for loading are in compliance, Labels, Pallet Moisture, Moisture Bags, Fumigation, Ratchet Bolt Belts etc. are in place as agreed between factory and client.
