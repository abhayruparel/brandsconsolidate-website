About Us

Brands Consolidated is an “IAF accredited ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company” engaged in Quality Inspections of Ceramic Porcelain Tiles and bathware collection including Kitchen sinks and other accessories in India since 2018.

We are a versatile team of experienced Technocrats and Veterans from Building Material Industry based in Ceramic Cluster of Morbi, Gujarat with rich hands-on experience of over 30 years in Ceramic Tiles Industry.

Morbi, Gujarat is one of the largest ceramic manufacturing cluster which offers a plethora of manufacturers producing various sizes and finishes for Wall, Floor, External, Cladding, Counter Top and Table Tops.

The developed markets globally require assurance, compliance for quality and inherent critical checkpoints.

We are sensitized to the gap in professional service for the fast-developing export market landscape for ceramic tiles in India.

A young professional team catering to your professional needs for quality assurance and control, assisting and optimizing best in class sourcing of various products.

We act as a guardian to overlook Quality and Sourcing Operations.

We are thankful to and acknowledge our patrons who over the last four years for the faith placed in our team to serve them.
